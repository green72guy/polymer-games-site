Hello World.

Welcome to the first ever blog post by Polymer Games, and the first devlog for Polymer! Polymer Games is a tiny indie game studio currently consisting of myself (Ahnaf) and a digital artist who I hope to introduce sometime soon.

We like to think of our first game Polymer as a mix between Snake and the bullet hell genre, as you'll be trying to survive against waves of enemies and obstacles while collecting orbs and growing your Polymer. As you can see in the video below, Polymer is currently at a VERY early stage of development- we'll be showing off a lot of additions in the future, including enemies, powerups, and graphical updates. This post is only a limited introduction to the very basic concept of the game.

Polymer is being developed in our own engine, Diamond, which is also a work in progress. We decided to create our own engine so that we would have full control over our technology stack and be able to optimize the performance (which will be important when we add fancier effects and features). Unlike Polymer, Diamond is open source and can be found on Github.


If you think this game might have some potential, please subscribe to our blog! You can also follow us on Twitter, like our page on Facebook, or subscribe to our YouTube channel!
